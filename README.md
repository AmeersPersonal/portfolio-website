## 🤖 AI as a Development Assistant

To optimize my development workflow and focus on core engineering challenges, I integrated AI into my process as a "pair programmer" and assistant. Here is how I leveraged it:

* **Boilerplate Reduction:** Instead of manually typing out repetitive component structures, I used AI to scaffold the initial boilerplate. This allowed me to immediately jump into writing custom logic and unique styling.
* **"Rubber Duck" Debugging:** When stuck on a tricky responsive design quirk or an unexpected console error, I used AI to bounce ideas off of and talk through my logic, helping me pinpoint the root cause much faster.
* **Copy Editing & Refinement:** I wrote the core messaging, project descriptions, and "About Me" sections manually to ensure they authentically represented my voice. Afterward, I used AI as a proofreader to tighten the phrasing and improve the overall flow.
* **Interactive Documentation:** Rather than getting lost in dense library documentation, I treated AI as an interactive tutor to quickly surface specific syntax examples and API integration methods, significantly accelerating my learning curve.
* **Design Brainstorming:** I used AI to help brainstorm complementary color palettes and typography pairings before translating my final decisions into CSS.
